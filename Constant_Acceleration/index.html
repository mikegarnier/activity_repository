Constant Acceleration Simulation

Instructions:
Press Play to start the ball rolling down the ramp.
Click "Mark Time & Distance" at least 5 times while the ball is accelerating.
Use your data to complete the lab. The in-class version used feet, but this simulation uses meters.

Dependent Variable: Distance traveled (m) — Y-axis
