Bohr Model Simulation: Click for Instructions
  • Assume that all atoms have a neutral charge
    (proton# = electron#).
  • Click on an element on the right once to highlight it.
  • Build the Bohr Model of that neutral atom.
  • Drag red dots into the nucleus to represent protons.
  • Drag white dots into the nucleus to represent neutrons.
  • Drag blue dots to rings to place electrons.
  • Electrons will have placement markers as reminders.
  • Once you think you've created the correct Bohr Model click the highlighted element again to see the correct version.
  • Compare your work to the card's model.
  • Click this box to close.
